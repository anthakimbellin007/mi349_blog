My Morning Routine

published on September 26, 2025.

In my Morning Routine at college, I wake up at 7:30 am. I also brush my teeth, and get ready for my first class. My first class at Michigan State University is CSE 231 at 9:00 am. I wash my face with my daily facial cleanser and then I take shower. Next, I get out the shower, dry off, and find some nice clothes to put on.I also pack my gym bag . I then, make my way to my car and drive to campus which is roughly at 10 minue drive. By the time I get to campus, it’s usually about 8 am. With an hour of time to spare, I usually will stop by the dining hall to potentially eat breakfast. Depending on the day and dining hall, there are usually a different variety of breakfast items to choose from. On this Tuesday morning, I decided to go to Brody Hall in Brody Neighboorhoodd to eat breakfast. After breakfast, I decide to purchase a snack from Sparty’s a exclusive store located on the first floor of brody square. I purchase a Combo X which is a combo of three items that comes with a beverage, a protein item, and a snack item. I decided to get a to go water bottle, a cup of cereal and pretzels to take with me to class. After this, I make my way to class to Wilson hall.

After Classes Routinue & Study Schedule

To continue, at Michigan State University, my class schedule consists of 2 more person classes. With this being said, the classes are MI 401 Topics in Information science at 12:00 pm and MI 349 Web Design and Devlopment at 3:00 pm. After going to all my classes, it is usually about 5:00 pm. I make my way to the IM West gym on campus and usually spen roughly an hour max at the gym. I would usually stretch,do cardio, do weights, and reps of sit ups to strengthen my core. After about an hour, i would leave and go to the dining hall to eat some food. The options have of course changed since it is now dinner time. At the dining hall I decided to eat dinner and get dessert and then leave. I then walk to my car and drive back to my apartment. When I arrive at my apartment, I take a shower, get out, and then begin reviewing my planner. Scheduled for 7:30 - 9:30 pm, I study, work on, and complete any work that i can get done within this time period as I’m usually very productive around this time along with the morning. After my study session, I review my tasks for the next day, brush my teeth and go to bed.
